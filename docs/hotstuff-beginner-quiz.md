# HotStuff – Beginner Onboarding Quiz

This quiz is created to help beginners understand the basics of the HotStuff
consensus protocol in a simple way.

---

## Q1. What is HotStuff mainly used for?
A. NFT minting  
B. Blockchain consensus  
C. Crypto trading  
D. Wallet security  

**Correct:** B  
**Explanation:** HotStuff is a Byzantine Fault Tolerant (BFT) consensus protocol
used to reach agreement in blockchains.

---

## Q2. What problem does HotStuff improve compared to older BFT protocols?
A. High energy consumption  
B. Slow finality and complex communication  
C. High gas fees  
D. Token inflation  

**Correct:** B  
**Explanation:** HotStuff reduces communication overhead and improves performance.

---

## Q3. Which feature makes HotStuff scalable?
A. Proof of Work  
B. Linear communication  
C. Central authority  
D. Mining rewards  

**Correct:** B  
**Explanation:** Linear communication makes HotStuff more efficient at scale.

---

## Q4. What does finality mean in HotStuff?
A. Transaction fee calculation  
B. A block cannot be reverted once confirmed  
C. Faster wallet sync  
D. Token burning  

**Correct:** B  
**Explanation:** Finality ensures confirmed blocks are irreversible.

---

## Q5. Who benefits from understanding HotStuff?
A. Developers  
B. Researchers  
C. Infrastructure builders  
D. All of the above  

**Correct:** D
